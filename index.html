Frequently asked questions

Do I need to buy my own lights?

Nope! Beautiful commercial-grade lights are included with every package. We design, provide, and hang them all for you.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Burleson and the surrounding Johnson, Tarrant, and Parker counties, including Fort Worth, Mansfield, Cleburne, Weatherford, Aledo, and more.

Do you take the lights down too?

Yes, and it's included in every package. After the holidays we take everything down and store your lights until next season, at no extra charge.

Are there any hidden fees?

None. Your quote covers it all: custom design, commercial-grade LED lights, professional install, in-season service, takedown, and storage. If you'd ever like to add on extras, we'll always talk it through with you first.

When should I book?

Early! Our calendar fills up fast in the fall. Reach out any time and we'll get you on the schedule.

How much does it cost?

Every home is different, so we give free, no-pressure quotes. Fill out the form or call and we'll put together an estimate for you.
